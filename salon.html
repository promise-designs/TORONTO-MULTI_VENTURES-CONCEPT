About Toronto Unisex Classic Salon

Look Your Best

Toronto Unisex Classic Salon is your premier destination for professional hair and beauty services. We offer expert haircuts, styling, coloring, and treatments for both men and women. Our skilled professionals use only the best products and techniques to ensure you leave looking and feeling your absolute best.

Located at Zone E Block 18 Shop 7&8, after A&G Print, OAU Central Market, close to Ojaja Hostel, Ile-Ife, we are easily accessible and ready to serve you. Walk in or book ahead — we make you shine.
